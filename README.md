# 🌱 EcoShop

EcoShop is a full-stack web application that promotes sustainable shopping by helping users discover eco-friendly products, local vendors, and environmentally responsible alternatives. The platform encourages conscious purchasing through environmental impact insights, a sustainability rewards program, and a community-driven recommendation system.

---

## 📖 Overview

Consumers often find it difficult to identify genuinely sustainable products and understand the environmental impact of their purchases. EcoShop addresses this challenge by providing a centralized platform where users can explore eco-friendly products, calculate their environmental footprint, earn rewards for sustainable choices, and engage with a community focused on green living.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌿 Eco-Friendly Product Catalog

- Browse verified eco-friendly products
- Search and filter products by category
- Explore sustainable alternatives
- View product details and sustainability information

### 🏪 Local Vendor Directory

- Discover local sustainable vendors
- Browse vendor listings
- Support environmentally responsible businesses

### 🌍 Environmental Impact Calculator

- Estimate the environmental impact of purchases
- Compare sustainable products with conventional alternatives
- Learn how purchasing decisions affect the environment

### 🎁 Sustainability Rewards Program

- Earn reward points for eco-friendly purchases
- Track sustainability milestones
- Redeem points for discounts or environmental initiatives

### ⭐ Community Reviews & Recommendations

- Submit product reviews and ratings
- Read community recommendations
- Share sustainable shopping experiences
